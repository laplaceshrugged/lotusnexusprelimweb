The Regenerative Herd

An AI-Guided Silvopasture Masterclass

Turn Your Livestock Into Partners for Healing the Land.

This masterclass goes beyond basic animal husbandry. It's a deep dive into the art and science of silvopasture—the integration of trees and grazing animals—augmented by modern technology. Learn how to use data to understand animal behavior and its direct impact on soil health, creating a truly regenerative system.

Course Curriculum

This foundational module explores the "why" behind regenerative grazing. We'll discuss the ethics of a non-slaughter promise and how to view your animals not as commodities, but as vital partners in building a healthier ecosystem.

Lessons Include:
  • Principles of Regenerative Grazing
  • The Role of Livestock in Soil Building
  • Animal Welfare as the Cornerstone of a Healthy Farm

Learn the time-tested "tradecraft" of moving animals to mimic natural patterns. This module covers the practical skills needed to set up and manage pastures for maximum regenerative impact.

Lessons Include:
  • Paddock Design and Fencing on a Budget
  • Calculating Forage and Rest Periods
  • Silvopasture Basics: Integrating Trees for Shade and Forage

A deep dive into the "Strategy." Learn how to use modern tools to listen to your herd. This module covers the practical setup and use of the technology that powers our AI-guided approach.

Lessons Include:
  • Choosing and Applying Smart Tags
  • Understanding Biometric and GPS Data
  • Setting up a Simple Data Dashboard

This is where the LOTUS philosophy comes together. We'll show you how our AI model interprets the data from the smart tags to suggest optimal grazing paths that build soil and respect the animals' natural behaviors.

Lessons Include:
  • Basics of Our AI Grazing Algorithm
  • Translating Data into a Daily Grazing Plan
  • Case Study: How AI Helped Us Restore a Degraded Pasture

The final step is connecting the data back to the real world. Learn to see the changes in your own pasture, confirming what the data is telling you and honing your own intuitive "Legacy of Tradecraft."

Lessons Include:
  • Simple Soil Health Tests You Can Do at Home
  • Identifying Key Forage Species and Their Health
  • Creating a Feedback Loop Between Data, Observation, and Action

The Problem: A High Barrier to Entry

Fenestration software is powerful, but mastering it is a steep and often isolating climb.

The tools that drive building energy analysis are complex and intimidating for students and entry-level professionals, creating a critical skills gap in a vital industry.

The Fenestra Vision: Education Through a New Lens

The most profound strategy isn't just to teach the tool, but to inspire the next generation of innovators.

Born from a Gen Z high schooler's vision, Fenestra is an educational platform that demystifies fenestration analysis. It builds foundational skills for LBNL's THERM & WINDOW software through engaging, accessible, and practical learning modules.

The Fenestra Learning Core

A suite of educational tools designed to build confidence and competence in fenestration analysis.

Interactive Modules

Step-by-step video tutorials and hands-on exercises for LBNL's THERM and WINDOW software.

Real-World Projects

Apply your new skills to practical building energy analysis case studies, from residential homes to commercial buildings.

Skill Certification

Earn shareable certificates as you master new competencies, validating your skills for potential employers.

Community Access

Connect with peers, ask questions, and learn from industry mentors in a dedicated online community.

Opening Windows of Opportunity

Fenestra is designed to build a pipeline of talent for the entire fenestration industry.

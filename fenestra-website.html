The Problem: A Widening Skills Gap

Sustainable design has a bottleneck most people don't see.

Specialized skills in both component analysis (using tools like LBNL's THERM & WINDOW) and whole-building energy modeling (using software like eQuest) are essential for sustainable design. Yet, these fields are often overlooked in education, creating a workforce gap that delays innovation.

The Vision: Turning Complexity into Career Opportunity

We believe the path to a high-impact career in sustainable design should be clear and accessible.

Born from a Gen Z founder’s passion to solve this exact problem, Fenestra is an educational platform that builds careers. We create a direct pathway into the most vital specialties in sustainable design, turning a complex topic into a career opportunity by demystifying the world of building performance analysis.

Our Guiding Philosophy

Our vision is guided by a deep respect for the industry's past and a clear strategy for its future.

Legacy Of Tradecraft

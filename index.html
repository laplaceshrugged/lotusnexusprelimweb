Our Philosophy

A Nexus of Innovation and Purpose.

As a Benefit Corporation, our legal charter binds us to a higher standard of purpose, accountability, and transparency. We are committed to considering the impact of our decisions on our workers, customers, suppliers, community, and the environment.

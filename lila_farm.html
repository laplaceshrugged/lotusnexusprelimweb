Pasture-Raised Eggs

From our happy flock in the Sunrise Coop. Our chickens live on pasture, resulting in eggs with deep golden yolks and incredible flavor.

The Story Behind Our Eggs

The Home

Our chickens live in a secure, clean coop with a solar-powered door that opens at sunrise and closes at sunset, respecting their natural rhythms.

The Diet

Our hens enjoy organic feed supplemented by a rich diet of grasses, legumes, and insects they forage for in their spacious, protected run.

The Purpose

Our chickens are a vital part of our farm's nutrient cycle, creating "black gold" compost that enriches our pastures and gardens.

The Result

A happy, healthy life results in a more nutritious and delicious egg with a deep, golden yolk. It's a difference you can see and taste.

Artisanal Honey

A direct taste of our farm's health. Our raw honey is produced by our AI-monitored bee colonies, which pollinate the diverse flora of our regenerative farm.

The Four Pillars of Our Artisanal Honey

The Source

Our honey is a liquid expression of the farm's unique, regenerative ecosystem, creating a complex, one-of-a-kind flavor profile.

The Process

Our honey is raw and unfiltered. It is never pasteurized, preserving delicate enzymes and beneficial properties, with natural pollen intact.

The Care

AI-powered, in-hive sensors allow for non-invasive stewardship, leading to stronger, healthier colonies.

The Transparency

Each jar can be traced to our apiary via the Open-Data Portal. Scan a QR code to see the health data of the colonies that produced your honey.

Neighbor's Farm

Where Our Journey Began

The foundation of our herd was made possible by two key members of our community, JR and Paige. They worked with us to coordinate the weaning of our first two calves—Zazu, a bull from Paige's farm, and Clover, a heifer from JR's. By selling us these exceptional animals, they showed their trust in our vision and gave us the perfect start.

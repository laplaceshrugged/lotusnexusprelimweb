Course Curriculum

Before drones, the best farmers had an intuitive ability to see problems before they started. This module explores that essential "tradecraft" of observation and how the Sentinel Drone acts as a modern extension of our own senses.

Lessons Include:
  • The History of Farm Scouting
  • Training Your Eye: What to Look For on the Ground
  • How Drones Augment, Not Replace, Intuition

A practical, hands-on guide to the essentials of safe and effective drone operation for agricultural purposes.

Lessons Include:
  • Choosing the Right Drone and Multispectral Camera
  • Understanding FAA Regulations and Safety Protocols
  • Automated Flight Planning for Consistent Data Collection

Learn to see the world as your Sentinel does. This module is a deep dive into the science of multispectral imaging and how different light bands reveal the hidden health of your plants.

Lessons Include:
  • The Science of NDVI and Plant Health Signatures
  • Calibrating Your Camera for Accurate Data
  • Stitching Images into a Cohesive Farm Map

This is where the LOTUS Nexus engine comes to life. Learn how our AI processes your raw image data and transforms it into simple, intuitive "health maps" that pinpoint problem areas.

Lessons Include:
  • Uploading Your Data to the Saffire Cloud
  • How the AI Identifies Patterns of Stress
  • Reading and Interpreting Your Pasture Health Maps

The final and most important step. This module teaches you how to use your new, data-rich insights to make targeted, regenerative interventions that heal your land.

Lessons Include:
  • Precision Application of Organic Amendments
  • Integrating Health Maps with Your Grazing Plan
  • Tracking Your Progress: Measuring Soil Health Over Time

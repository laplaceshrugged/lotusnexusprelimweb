From First Principles to Real-World Impact

I believe that the most complex challenges—whether in a physics problem or a business plan—can be understood by breaking them down to their fundamental truths. This approach has been the guiding force behind my academic pursuits and my entrepreneurial ventures.

My goal is to be a bridge between the abstract and the applied: to take the elegant logic of mathematics and code, combine it with the hands-on grit of building something real, and create systems that are not only innovative but also conscientious. From teaching pre-algebra to designing AI for regenerative farms, my work is driven by a mission to build, to educate, and to leave a positive mark.

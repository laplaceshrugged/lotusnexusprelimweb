The Problem: A Labyrinth of Regulation

Building codes are the bedrock of safety, yet navigating them is a maze of complexity.

Manual compliance review is slow, prone to human error, and a major source of project delays and budget overruns. Every revision cycle costs valuable time and resources.

The Structura Solution: Certainty by Design

The most profound strategy is not to guess at compliance, but to build it into the design process.

Structura is an AI-powered system from LOTUS Nexus that automates building code compliance review. It empowers architects, engineers, and regulators to design and approve with confidence, clarity, and unprecedented speed.

The Structura AI Core

A suite of AI-powered tools designed to bring clarity and efficiency to the entire compliance lifecycle.

Instant Code Analysis

Upload architectural plans and receive a comprehensive compliance report in minutes, not weeks.

Jurisdiction-Specific AI

Our AI models are trained on local, state, and international building codes for precise, context-aware reviews.

Collaborative Platform

A shared workspace for architects, engineers, and reviewers to identify and resolve compliance issues efficiently.

Audit Trail & Reporting

Automatically generate comprehensive compliance reports and maintain a detailed history of all reviews and revisions.

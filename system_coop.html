The Sunrise Coop

An automated, solar-powered system for raising healthier, happier chickens with less labor and more respect.

Traditional chicken coops require daily manual intervention and often fail to protect flocks from predators. The Sunrise Coop is Saffire's solution: a resourceful, intelligent system that automates the daily rhythms of the coop, ensuring the flock is safe, secure, and lives in harmony with the sun.

Key Features

Automated Solar Door

A light-sensitive, solar-powered door automatically opens at sunrise and closes securely at sunset, protecting the flock from nocturnal predators without any daily labor.

Resourceful Design

Based on proven tradecraft, the coop is built with simple materials for maximum durability, ventilation, and predator resistance, ensuring a safe and healthy environment.

Nutrient Cycling Hub

The coop is designed for easy collection of nitrogen-rich manure and bedding, creating "black gold" compost that is a vital fertilizer for the entire farm.

Focus on Well-being

A spacious, protected run allows the flock to express their natural behaviors—foraging, dust bathing, and enjoying fresh air—leading to happier, healthier birds.

Proven in the Field.

The Sunrise Coop at Lila Farm is not a concept; it's a success story. It's a key part of our "Respect for Life" philosophy and the secret behind our delicious, nutrient-dense pasture-raised eggs.

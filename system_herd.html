Smart Herd Management

Turn your livestock into active partners in land regeneration with our AI-guided grazing system.

Traditional grazing can lead to soil degradation and requires constant, labor-intensive management. The Saffire Smart Herd Management system is a revolutionary approach that transforms livestock into a powerful asset for ecological restoration. By combining real-time data with AI-powered insights, we empower farmers to manage their herds with unparalleled precision and empathy.

Key Features

Biometric Smart Tags

Lightweight, non-invasive ear tags monitor key health indicators like body temperature and movement, providing early warnings for potential health issues.

GPS & Pasture Mapping

Track herd movement in real-time to understand grazing patterns, prevent overgrazing, and ensure even distribution of manure for natural fertilization.

AI-Powered Grazing Plans

Our LOTUS Nexus AI synthesizes herd data with pasture health maps from the Saffire Sentinel to recommend daily, optimized grazing rotations that maximize soil regeneration.

Animal Welfare Monitoring

The system provides alerts for abnormal behavior, allowing for proactive, preventative care that reduces stress and improves the overall health of the herd.

Proven in the Field.

The Smart Herd Management system at Lila Farm is not a concept; it's a success story. It has been instrumental in the rapid restoration of our pastures and is the core of our "Respect for Life" philosophy.
